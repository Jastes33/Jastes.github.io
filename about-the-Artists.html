Our Story

everything started for us July 2022, it was the first con for Tonic and Jastes together and it was a weekend that changed everything. Jastes, being new to the fandom was very nervous and unsure of what to expect, that is not the case anymore. Tonic!, acting under a different name back then was very experience in how to make the most of a con but Tonic! felt somewhat left out by not being able to make the most of the fursuit activities. After this both of us set out to make our own fursuits so that we could e​xpress ourselves and imerse ourselves in the fandom. This journey,  for us, unlocked a new passions. The skills that we learned and the bond that were formed have shown us that this is something that we should do together.

Tonic!

Just a fun loving Yeen who like making Fursuits.

Jastes

II officially joined the fandom in 2021 and very shortly after purchased my first partial just in time for CanFur 2022. The experience changed my life and made me realize that this was something that i wanted to be a part of. I spent the next year making my first suit. I also went to several cons and became involved in content creation. You can find me on TikTok and Twitch
